The experience offered by the application is determined by its ablity to communicate with the Autographer seamlessly. To that end, considerable effort was spent creating a robust framework on which the application rests. This communication framework (based on [Bluetooth](https://developer.apple.com/bluetooth/)) was designed from the ground-up alongside OMG Life engineers, ensuring each aspect was properly documented and tested. One of my favourite aspects of the Autographer app is the ability to create a video or GIF of images mixed with your favourite music in a custom editor. The images, each with location and sensor data, are transferred on-demand from the accessory via Bluetooth. {% endfilter %} {% endblock body %}
