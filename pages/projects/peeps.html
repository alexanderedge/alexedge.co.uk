[Tom Cavill](https://twitter.com/tomcavill) and I released Peeps in July 2014, since then it has been featured by Apple and as App of the Week in ShortList magazine. I developed both the iOS and server components of Peeps employing, among many other technologies, push notifications, third-party SMS integration, phone number verification, video compression and iOS 8.0 animation APIs. To date, approximately 30% of the iOS app is written in Swift.
